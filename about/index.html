About Alienonyx

Alienonyx, Inc. is a fabless semiconductor company developing cryogenic analog and mixed-signal ASICs, beginning with integrated readout for superconducting single-photon detector arrays. We pair deep memory-circuit and mixed-signal design experience with a modern, open-source silicon design flow — and a conviction that the quantum and superconducting-detector industry deserves components, not just publications.

Founder

Alienonyx was founded by Ali Al-Shamma, an analog and mixed-signal integrated-circuit designer with nearly three decades of experience across the memory and semiconductor industry — including senior design roles at Intel, Western Digital, SanDisk, AMD, and Matrix Semiconductor. His work spans sense amplifiers, data converters, precision references, on-chip power and clock generation, and compute-in-memory and neuromorphic circuits, and he has led test-chip IP design and first-silicon bring-up. He is a named inventor on 30+ U.S. patents in memory and mixed-signal design, has published at ISSCC, and holds an MSc and BSc in Electrical Engineering from Santa Clara University. Alienonyx’s readout architecture is new and independently developed, and patent-pending.
